About Me

Hi! I'm Jack.

My name is Jack Turner, I run Animatronic Warehouse. I was captivated by the attention to detail, artistry, and music of the Rock-afire Explosion when I was 12 years old. In the years since my introduction to that show, I have been restoring animatronics for private owners and businesses nonstop. I do artistic, mechanical, and electronic work for the restorations. Some work is delegated out to a talented team of people, so that quality is ensured. I have restored and sold over 50 animatronic characters, painted dozens of masks, hands, feet, and have worked towards making parts available for fans so they can restore characters with the resources necessary. In 2023, I finished my largest project, the restoration of an entire Rock-afire Explosion show. The restoration included the total mechanical overhauling of each character, the recreation of every single cosmetic piece, the building of a stage, air supply set up, and many many other facets. With it's completion, fans of the Rock-afire Explosion can now see the entire restored show in person.

After 4 years of intense restoration, I am happy to announce that The Rock-afire Explosion is now viewable at Animatronics Warehouse. Every character and prop has been meticulously restored, making the show appear as though it was just installed in a Showbiz Pizza Place. No other animatronic experience in the world compares to this one. Once the lights dim and the music plays, you’ll understand why The Rock-afire Explosion is considered the greatest animatronic band ever. This tour is not just the best animatronic experience, but the greatest and truest Rock-afire Explosion experience.

During the two hour tour, you will be enlightened on the history behind the show, how the restoration process occurred, and be able to listen to your favorite shows. Following this, you will be given a tour of the warehouse and see where historical Creative Engineering artifacts and other animatronic projects are stored.
